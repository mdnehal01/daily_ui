Slow and steady is the best process of gaining physique. Just be consistent. To achieve slow and steady growth in the gym, it's important to focus on progressive overload, which involves gradually increasing the weight, reps, or intensity of your workouts over time.




If you want to train like a champ in the gym, you need to have a mindset and approach similar to that of a professional athlete. This means committing to your workouts, setting challenging goals, and pushing yourself to reach your full potential.

Set specific and measurable goals: Define what you want to achieve in the gym, and make sure your goals are challenging but realistic.
